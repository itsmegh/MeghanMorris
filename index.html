About Me

I am a user-focused Web Developer with more than ten years of experience as a strategic writer and marketing-communicator. These experiences, combined with a certificate from the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at University of Denver, an MBA degree and undergraduate degree in Journalism, have allowed me to appreciate a variety of innovative, challenging environments where I thrive as a professional.

My unique perspective allows me to dig deeper to create better online user experiences. I am an avid learner and enjoy collaborating with colleagues to create mission driven web applications. I am knowledgeable in areas such as: full stack development, healthcare, natural products, finance, print and online media, NGOs and sustainable business.

I look forward to hearing from you!

Conscious Cuisine

A team project for University of Denver's Coding Bootcamp.

The first team project gave us the opportunity to integrate information from APIs into a web application. We chose to create a nutrition based application allowed users to search for an ingredient and retrieve recipe ideas with nutrition information included for each result. We used the Yummly and Edamam APIs. The included version is my redesign using Bootstrap.
